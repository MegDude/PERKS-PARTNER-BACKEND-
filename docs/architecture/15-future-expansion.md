# Volume 15: Future Expansion

## Purpose

Define extensibility paths for new cities, enterprise deployments, native mobile, public APIs, marketplace services, and partner integrations.

## Expansion Targets

- Additional districts and cities
- Multi-property portfolio support
- White-label workspaces
- Enterprise partner APIs
- Native mobile app
- Offline QR support
- Marketplace listings
- Predictive recommendations
- City-scale knowledge graph

## Architecture Rule

Future expansion must consume the Master Registry Engine and Platform Services. New cities, partner categories, providers, and agents should be configuration-driven where possible.
